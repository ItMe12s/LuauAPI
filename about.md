# LuauAPI

**Write Geode mods in Luau!**

---

## This mod offers

- Easy to learn syntax.
- Luau LSP autocompletion.
- JIT and JIT-less support.
- No more waiting for your mods to compile.
- Basically write a whole Geode mod like it's a Roblox game.
